Frogger

Controls

  • a - left
  • d - right
  • w - up
  • s - down

Legend

  • Green Circle = Frog
  • White Rectangle = Car
  • Red Rectangle = Turtle
  • Brown Rectangle = Plank
  • Green with White on left Rectangle = Crocodile
  • White part on left of Green rectangle = Crocdile Mouth

Game rules

  • Moving up on road and not colliding with car will earn 10 points
  • Moving up to safezone will earn 10 points
  • Moving up and landing on a moving object on river will earn 10 points
  • Moving up and landing in distinct target area will earn 100 points
  • If frog collides with car on road, move into the river or move into
    crocodile mouth, frog will die and return to starting point
  • To complete 1 round, 5 frogs must land in the distinct target areas
    and each distinct target area only allows 1 frog to land in it
  • It is possible to move down and left and right when frog is in a
    a moving object in river but moving till the frog is in the river will cause
    the frog to die and return to starting point
  • Once a round is over all 5 frogs are removed from the distinct target
    areas and a frog is created at starting point and the new round starts
  • For every next round the game becomes harder as the speed of moving
    objects will increase
  • Turtles will dive and frog cannot land on turtle when it dives
